UX Fails

HEB Curbside

I was placing an order for pickup for my groceries on what I considered a "Saturday night". I wanted to place an order for pickup for Sunday morning. However, because of the technicality of it meing past midnight, it automatically considered Sunday as "today" and Monday as "tomorrow". Humans don't think this way and there's no easy way to change the date once you've placed an order. I had to cancel and re-start my order at this point, which was a huge inconvenience. Making it clearer to the user what day they're placing the order for would be ideal, as "today" and "tomorrow" isn't clear enough. Something more along the lines of "Today, Sunday August 20th" would help a lot as it would give the user several more cues that are necessary at times.
